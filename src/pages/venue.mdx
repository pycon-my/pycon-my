## Driving here


You may park your car around the campus. List of parking nearby is available [here](https://maps.app.goo.gl/HZ8Akj2AuRyWPzdE7?g_st=iw).

The parking rate is as follows:

- **Sunway University Basement Parking**

  First 2 hours: RM2.00

  Subsequent hour: RM1.00

  Maximum charge per entry: RM5.00

- **BRT Complex (Park and Ride)** _(Opposite Monash University)_

  First 3 hours: RM3.00

  Subsequent hour: RM1.00

  Maximum charge per entry: RM7.00

  Note: Non-TnG cards are subject to a 10% fee
